# WhatsApp Recording Examples

Use these natural language examples to record various transactions through WhatsApp (Text or Voice).

## 1. Sales (Money coming in)
- "Sold a laptop for 70,000 with 18% GST"
- "Sale of 5 keyboards to Apollo Systems at 1,500 each"
- "B2B sale to 36AAICA8527D1ZP for 12,000"

## 2. Purchases (Stock/Inventory bought)
- "Bought 50 monitors from Dell for 5,00,000"
- "Purchase invoice from SV Exports for 8,500"
- "Inventory purchase for 25,000 at 12% GST"

## 3. Expenses (Operational costs)
- "Paid 5,000 for office rent"
- "Spent 1,200 on electricity bill today"
- "1,500 for tea and snacks for staff"
- "Internet bill 850 paid via UPI"

## 4. Payments (Settling dues/Salary/Subscriptions)
The bot will follow up to ask if these are **Single** or **Recurring**.
- "Paid salary to Sridhar 25,000"
- "Paid vendor 15,000 for last month's inventory"
- "Monthly subscription for software 999 paid"
- "Recurring rent payment of 12,000 starting today"

---
### Pro Tip:
If you send a **photo** of a bill, the bot will automatically ask you to select the category (Sale, Purchase, Expense, or Payment) using buttons.
